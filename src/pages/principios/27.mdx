
Living networks can be studied with a magnifying glass or from a lookout. A crisis-stricken forest where the swamp rots, toucans get sick, and insects devour what is left can be studied in two ways. The specialist approach looks at each issue with a magnifying glass and sees intricate, independent, complex, multicausal, and apparently irresolvable problems. From the lookout, one sees a single simple cause: deforestation.
